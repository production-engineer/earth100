Earth100 Rules

RULE 01

25% CAGR Minimum

Companies growing slower than 25% annually are examined with extra scrutiny. High growth is a prerequisite for outsized impact at scale.

RULE 02

Sustainable Future

Mission and goals must be actively moving humanity toward a more sustainable world. Not just neutral — directionally positive.

RULE 03

Clear Growth Path

Every company must demonstrate a credible path: sales velocity → channel expansion → geographic/product expansion → exit or moat formation.
